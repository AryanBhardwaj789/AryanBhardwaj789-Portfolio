Coffee Express

Coffee Express is a user-friendly JavaFX application designed to revolutionize the way coffee lovers order their favorite beverages. With an intuitive interface and a plethora of customization options, Coffee Express offers a seamless and delightful coffee ordering experience.

Features:
Customization: Choose from a variety of coffee types, sizes, and toppings to create your perfect cup of coffee.
Real-time Price Updates: See the price of your order adjust as you make your selections, ensuring transparency and accuracy.
Multiple Screens: Navigate through different screens, including login, signup, order, payment, and receipt screens, for a smooth ordering process.
Data Validation: Ensure that your inputs, such as quantity and payment information, are accurate and valid.
File Handling: Store order details and transaction records for easy access and order history tracking.


Technologies Used:
JavaFX: For the frontend user interface and application logic.
FXML: For defining the application's UI layout.
File Handling: For storing and managing order details and transaction records.
Polymorphism: Utilized through interfaces and abstract classes to achieve dynamic behavior.


How to Use:[GITHUB]
To experience Coffee Express, simply clone the repository and run the Main.java file in your preferred Java IDE. Explore the various coffee options, customize your order, and enjoy a freshly brewed cup of coffee in no time!


Future Enhancements:
In the future, Coffee Express aims to integrate additional features such as user profiles, order history, and loyalty programs to further enhance the user experience. Stay tuned for exciting updates!
