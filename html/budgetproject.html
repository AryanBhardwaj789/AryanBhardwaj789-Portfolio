Budget Master

Budget Master is a UI Prototype designed to help you take control of your finances effortlessly. With its intuitive interface and powerful features, managing your budget has never been easier.

Key Features:
Custom Budget Categories: Create personalized budget categories to suit your financial goals.
Expense Tracking: Easily track your expenses and see where your money is going.
Budget Visualization: Visualize your budget with easy-to-understand charts and graphs.
Budget Goals: Set budget goals and track your progress towards achieving them.
Expense Reminders: Receive timely reminders for upcoming expenses.

Technologies Used:
Figma: Used for designing the user interface and user experience.

Project Duration:Around 14 weeks

Note:
Budget Master is a UI prototype design created on Figma.
